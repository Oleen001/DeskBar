# Asset provenance

## WhiteDog Lottie animations

The local-only JSON files under `Resources/WhiteDog/` come from these IconScout
packs downloaded by the repository owner under an IconScout Premium subscription:

- [White Dog Animation Pack](https://iconscout.com/lottie-animation-pack/white-dog-animation-pack_398936)
- [Cute White Dog Animation Pack](https://iconscout.com/lottie-animation-pack/cute-white-dog-animation-pack_398935)

The Cute White Dog pack page identifies the asset as a four-animation Lottie
pack, credits Haris Amrullah, and labels it Digital License. The White Dog
pack is also a four-animation Lottie pack. Keep the owner's IconScout download
receipt and subscription record with the project for license verification.

IconScout's Digital License allows use in software and other digital products,
but its restrictions prohibit redistributing the downloaded asset as a
standalone/source file. The JSON files are therefore intentionally excluded
from this public repository and are copied into the app only by the local build
script. Do not republish the raw JSON as a general-purpose asset pack or
template without confirming the exact license terms for the account and
download.

## Lottie framework

DeskBar uses the Lottie framework through the `lottie-spm` Swift Package
dependency. Its license and notices remain governed by that upstream project.
